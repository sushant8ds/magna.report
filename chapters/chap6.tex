\chapter{TESTING}

\justify

This chapter describes the testing strategy adopted for the HPDC Defect Detection System. Testing is structured at two levels: acceptance testing (validating end-to-end system performance against defined success criteria) and unit testing (validating individual pipeline modules).

\section{Test Plan and Test Cases}

\subsection*{Acceptance Test Plan}

The acceptance test plan validates that the complete system meets the performance and functional requirements defined in Chapter~2.

\midrule
ATP-01 & End-to-end detection on unseen HPDC images & At least one correct detection (Crack or Cold Shut) in defect-positive test images \\
\rowcolor{tableRowAlt}ATP-02 & mAP50-95 on test split $\geq$ 0.20 & EMA mAP50-95 $\geq$ 0.20 \\

\begin{findingbox}
\textbf{Testing Outcome:} All unit tests passed. The acceptance test ATP-02 was met with EMA mAP50-95 = 0.2379 on the Experiment 7 test split. The EMA checkpoint consistently outperformed the regular checkpoint across all three training experiments (ATP-04 confirmed).
